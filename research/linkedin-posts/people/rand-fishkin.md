### Citations vs. brand presence in AI answers

We see things like "Reddit dropped from 8% to 3% of citations in AI answers," and think that Reddit posts must therefore have less influence on the brands ChatGPT, Claude, or Google present in their responses... But that's not true! Citations are correlated, sure, but not causal, to which brands appear in AI answers (and that's further complicated by RAG vs. base model).

### Are SEO writers in trouble? Niche expertise + the person behind the prompt

This isn't intended to offend anyone but.... Are SEO writers in trouble?

I don't think they are IF they're experts in a certain niche.

Semrush published this data on how marketers are viewing AI content vs. human written content and it got a lot of people fired up.

Get the data here: https://lnkd.in/g4a3c8j4 -- You will see my take in there but here's an additional perspective:

It's all about the person behind the prompt and final content review.

If you tasked me with writing an SEO blog post about:

- Quantitative Easing (QE)
- CRISPR Gene Editing
- String Theory
- Cryogenics

It would probably be a mediocre post.

But if you tasked me to write an SEO blog post about something I know well...

I could probably write it with AI and deliver something better than the average writer. This is where I think the opportunity lies.

Arm great thinkers, writers and SMEs with AI tools to create great content faster.

This is the way. What do you think?

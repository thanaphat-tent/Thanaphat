### 5 AI SEO skills that are worth learning

5 AI SEO skills that are worth learning:

**1. Prove value**

Track 4 metrics for AI answers:

>> Is your brand mentioned?

>> If you're mentioned, what position?

>> How many platforms are you mentioned on?

Don't treat all platforms the same. A brand mention on ChatGPT is more valuable than Grok.

>> Are your brand mentions on the sources of retrieval?

This is a leading metric because it influences AI answers.

P.S. attribution is a nightmare right now. These KPIs aren't perfect but they can help.

⎯

**2. Control the narrative**

Start with your homebase (your website) to feed the AI correct inputs about your brand when direct retrieval is used.

At Rankability, we've found that direct retrieval (to a brand's website) is common for queries with the brand.

SHOCKER!

Most businesses are plagued with incorrect information spread across the Internet.

Use deep research from any AI to find inconsistencies.

⎯

**3. Build a knowledge base**

This is the single best way to create on-brand content if you're using AI.

You can use ChatGPT Projects if you're looking for a decent, simple solution.

If you're more hardcore, then use Client Brain in Rankability 2 (coming soon).

⎯

**4. Master link building 2.0**

Backlinks still matter because authoritative websites rank well in traditional search and search is a top retrieval source for AI.

But AI platforms don't care about followed, nofollow, or even sponsored links/mentions.

In fact, we've found that you don't even need a link!

What matters is that your brand is mentioned on the top sources of retrieval.

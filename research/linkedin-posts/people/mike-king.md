### Mike King on relevance engineering and the end of SEO as we know it

Danny Goodwin at Search Engine Land interviewed Michael King about AI in search and why Relevance Engineering (r17g) is becoming the cornerstone of modern SEO.

As Mike put it: "Search has always been a branding channel. We've never positioned it that way because it's always been considered like, performance - we drive traffic, we drive clicks."

Their conversation also covered:
- The evolution of Google's AI Overviews and what SEOs might be overlooking
- The concept of "query fan-out" and its implications for content strategy
- Why traditional SEO tools may be lagging behind Google's advancements

Mike's perspective is consistent - to stay ahead, we have to continue to adapt.

**Topic:** SERP personalization, SERP features, and what to track for the year ahead.

The type of personalizations that the SERPs will have, and the number and level of SERP features that will start showcasing, will only get higher. That is why one of the top recommendations for next year is: **if you have not yet started to track pixels, you should.** If you have not yet started to track the inclusion — increase or decrease — of SERP features and sub-features that are important in the SERPs for you, you should, and you should track those along with click-through rate and actual traffic and engagement that you get per topic, per area, per group of queries.

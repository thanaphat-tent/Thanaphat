# Rand Fishkin

## Rand Fishkin — Additional Information

Rand argues that marketers obsess over traffic, but the web is sending fewer clicks from nearly every platform: Google, social, YouTube, Reddit, and AI tools. He says traffic-first KPIs are becoming outdated.

### SEO Production Lesson

Don't create content only to chase visits.

Create content that drives:

- Brand recall
- Email signups
- Demand generation
- Direct traffic
- Assisted conversions

## Matt Diggity — Additional Materials

### AI Overviews SEO case study: clarity beats cleverness

Matt emphasizes that AI systems prefer **clear, direct, concise answers** over vague or overly clever writing styles.

He shows examples where **lower-ranked pages** were cited because they answered more clearly.

SEO Production Lesson:
- Use AI prompts that create **direct answers first**
- Use **simple language**
- Use **clear explanations**
- Ensure **immediate relevance**
- Avoid bloated intros and filler

### Programmatic SEO case study: templates + scale

Matt shared a programmatic SEO case study where templated page creation helped grow traffic and rankings, with hundreds of pages built efficiently.

SEO Production Lesson:

  - Comparison pages
